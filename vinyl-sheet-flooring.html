Vinyl Sheet Flooring

Seamless, Waterproof Vinyl Sheet Flooring

Heat-welded sheet vinyl installed to a commercial standard — the go-to choice for healthcare, washrooms, kitchens and any space that needs a hygienic, water-resistant floor across the GTA.

Overview

Why Choose Vinyl Sheet?

Vinyl sheet flooring comes in large continuous rolls, which means far fewer seams than tile-based products. When those seams are heat-welded — as we do on commercial installs — the floor becomes effectively seamless and impervious to water, making it one of the most hygienic and low-maintenance surfaces available.

It's the material of choice in hospitals, clinics, labs and commercial kitchens for exactly this reason, and it works beautifully in residential kitchens, bathrooms, laundry rooms and basements too. With more than 10 years installing sheet vinyl in demanding commercial environments, we bring that same precision to every project.
