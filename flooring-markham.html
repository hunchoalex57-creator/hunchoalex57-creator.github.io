Flooring in Markham

Flooring Installation in Markham

Markham blends heritage neighbourhoods like Unionville with a major corporate and tech corridor and the growing condos of Downtown Markham. Whether it's a family home, a high-end office or a condo unit, we install floors that look sharp and stand up to daily use.

Local Know-How

From Family Homes to Corporate Offices

Markham's residential side runs from the character homes of Unionville and Markham Village to newer family neighbourhoods like Cornell, Berczy Village, Wismer and Greensborough. In these homes, waterproof luxury vinyl plank and cozy carpet do the heavy lifting — practical, good-looking and built for busy households.

Markham is also one of the GTA's biggest business hubs, home to a dense tech and corporate corridor along Highway 404 and Woodbine. That means offices, clinics and retail that need flooring installed cleanly and often on tight after-hours schedules — carpet tile, VCT and sheet vinyl that stay professional under constant foot traffic. The Downtown Markham condos bring standard tower requirements we handle every day: certificates of insurance, elevator bookings and quiet hours.
