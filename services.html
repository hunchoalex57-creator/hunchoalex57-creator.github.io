Included With Every Job

Prep, Precision & a Clean Finish

No matter which material you choose, every Apex installation includes the fundamentals that separate a floor that lasts from one that fails early:

  • Subfloor assessment & moisture checking
  • Patching, skim-coating and self-leveling as needed
  • Proper adhesives and installation methods for the material
  • Tight seams, straight lines and clean edges
  • Full cleanup and a final walkthrough with you
