Flooring in Toronto

Flooring Installation in the City of Toronto

From high-rise condos in the core to century homes in the east end, Toronto floors come with their own challenges — tight elevator bookings, uneven old subfloors and strict condo noise rules. We handle all of it. Commercial-grade installation, right across the 416.

Local Know-How

We Know How Toronto Buildings Work

Installing in a downtown condo is a different job than a house in the suburbs. Property managers want a certificate of insurance before you set foot in the building, the service elevator has to be booked in advance, and most towers have delivery windows and noise-restricted hours. We've worked inside these constraints for years — so your install stays on schedule and on the right side of building management.

Toronto's older housing stock brings its own quirks. The semis and Victorians in areas like Leslieville, Riverdale, The Beaches and Roncesvalles often hide sloped, patched or squeaky subfloors under the old carpet. We assess and level properly before a single plank goes down, so your new floor sits flat and quiet — not bouncing over yesterday's problems.
