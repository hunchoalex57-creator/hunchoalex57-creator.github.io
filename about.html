About Us

Trade-Level Flooring, Brought Directly to You

Apex Resilient Flooring was founded on a simple idea: take more than a decade of commercial resilient flooring experience and offer it directly to GTA businesses, homeowners and property managers — with honest pricing and craftsmanship you can count on.

Our Story

Built on 10+ Years of Commercial Experience

For over ten years, our work has centred on commercial resilient flooring — the demanding, spec-driven installs found in offices, retail, schools, healthcare and institutional buildings across the Greater Toronto Area. That environment teaches you to do things properly: prep the substrate, hit the tolerances, weld tight seams and finish clean.

Apex Resilient Flooring exists to bring that same standard directly to customers. Whether it's a commercial buildout, a property-management turnover or a homeowner's basement, you get the same careful preparation and installation technique — without paying for layers of overhead.
