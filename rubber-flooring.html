Rubber Flooring

Rubber Flooring & Stair Treads

Slip-resistant, shock-absorbing rubber flooring and stair treads for gyms, stairwells and any space where safety and durability come first — installed across the GTA.

Overview

Safety, Grip & Longevity

Rubber flooring is built for demanding, safety-conscious environments. Its natural grip and cushioning make it the go-to surface for gyms and fitness centres, while rubber stair treads add slip resistance and wear protection to high-traffic stairwells in schools, offices and apartment buildings.

Available in tiles, rolls and moulded stair treads, rubber flooring is quiet, resilient and extremely long-lasting. Because it's often installed on stairs and busy landings, precise fitting and secure adhesion are critical for both safety and appearance — details we handle to a commercial standard.

Grip, cushioning and safety where it matters most
Key Benefits

Why Choose Rubber?

  • Slip-resistant — excellent grip for safety-critical areas.
  • Shock-absorbing — cushions impact in gyms and play areas.
  • Extremely durable — handles heavy, constant traffic.
  • Sound-dampening — reduces noise on stairs and floors.
  • Low maintenance — easy to clean and long-lasting.
  • Stair tread options — moulded treads with nosing for safety.
