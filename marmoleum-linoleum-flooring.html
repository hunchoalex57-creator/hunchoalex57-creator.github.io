Marmoleum & Linoleum

Eco-Friendly Marmoleum & Linoleum Flooring

Natural, low-VOC linoleum flooring that's tough, healthy and long-lasting — the trusted choice for schools, healthcare and green-focused builds across the GTA.

Overview

Natural Flooring That Lasts Decades

Marmoleum is a modern brand of linoleum made from natural raw materials — linseed oil, wood flour, jute and pine rosin. It's one of the most environmentally friendly resilient floors on the market: low-VOC, biodegradable and naturally anti-microbial, which is why it's specified so often for schools, hospitals and offices where indoor air quality matters.

Beyond the green credentials, linoleum is genuinely durable — properly installed and maintained, it can last 25 to 40 years. It comes in sheet and tile formats and a huge palette of colours, allowing for clean solid fields or eye-catching custom inlays. Correct subfloor prep and seaming technique are essential, and that's where our experience shows.

Made from natural materials — built to last decades
Key Benefits

Why Choose Marmoleum?

  • Eco-friendly & low-VOC — natural materials, great for indoor air quality.
  • Naturally anti-microbial — resists bacteria, ideal for healthcare.
  • Very long lifespan — 25–40 years with proper care.
  • Colour-through wear — colour runs through the material.
  • Design flexibility — solids, patterns and custom inlays.
  • Comfortable underfoot — warmer and quieter than tile.
