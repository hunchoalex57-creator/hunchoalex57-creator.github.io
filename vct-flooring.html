VCT Tile

Vinyl Composition Tile (VCT) Flooring

The workhorse of commercial flooring — durable, affordable and endlessly maintainable. We install and finish VCT for schools, retail and institutional spaces across the GTA.

Overview

Proven, Practical & Budget-Friendly

Vinyl composition tile has been the standard commercial floor for decades, and for good reason. It's one of the most cost-effective hard-surface options available, extremely durable under heavy traffic, and when properly maintained it can be stripped, waxed and buffed back to a like-new shine for many years.

VCT comes in a wide range of colours, which makes it easy to create borders, patterns, wayfinding and logos — common in schools, hospitals and retail. The keys to a lasting VCT floor are a properly prepped substrate, correct adhesive and tight, consistent tile spacing. We handle the full install and can apply initial finish coats so it's ready for traffic.

The durable, maintainable commercial classic
Key Benefits

Why Choose VCT?

  • Very cost-effective — excellent value for large commercial areas.
  • Extremely durable — built for schools, retail and institutions.
  • Renewable finish — strip, wax and buff back to new.
  • Design options — patterns, borders and inlaid logos.
  • Easy tile replacement — swap individual damaged tiles.
  • Widely available — easy to source and match.

How We Install VCT
