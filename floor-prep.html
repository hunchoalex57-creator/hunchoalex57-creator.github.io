Floor Prep & Leveling

Floor Prep, Self-Leveling & Subfloor Repair

The most important part of any flooring job is the part you never see. We prep substrates properly — moisture testing, patching and self-leveling — so your new floor lasts. Serving the GTA.

Overview

Why Floor Prep Makes or Breaks a Job

Even the best flooring material will fail if it's installed over a poor substrate. Bumps and low spots telegraph through vinyl and telegraph even worse over time; trapped moisture in concrete can break adhesive bonds and cause floors to lift; cracks and old adhesive residue prevent proper adhesion. That's why every quality install starts with proper preparation.

With over a decade of commercial experience, we treat floor prep as its own discipline. We assess and moisture-test the substrate, grind or scrape off old adhesives, patch cracks and damage, and use self-leveling compound to bring the floor flat and true. Get this right, and your finished floor looks better and lasts far longer.

Flat, dry, sound substrates — the base of every great floor
