Flooring in Vaughan

Flooring Installation in Vaughan

Vaughan is booming — from the brand-new condo towers rising at the Vaughan Metropolitan Centre to the large custom homes of Woodbridge and Kleinburg. We install durable, great-looking floors that match the pace and quality of one of the GTA's fastest-growing cities.

Local Know-How

Flooring for New Builds & Custom Homes

A lot of Vaughan is newer construction — the spacious detached homes across Maple, Sonoma Heights and Vellore Village where big open main floors call for a consistent, waterproof luxury vinyl plank that ties the whole space together. In the upscale homes of Kleinburg and Woodbridge, we bring the same commercial-grade prep and finishing to premium materials.

On the commercial side, Vaughan's growth means constant demand — new retail at Vaughan Mills, offices and medical space around the VMC, and the warehouses and units along Highway 7 and the 400. That's exactly where our commercial background fits: VCT, sheet vinyl and rubber built for real traffic. And the VMC condos come with the usual tower rules — insurance certificates, elevator bookings and noise windows — which we handle routinely.
